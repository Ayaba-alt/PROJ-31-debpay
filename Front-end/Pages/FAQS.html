Frequently Asked Questions

  • With My Debtors platform, you get to have your money paid directly to you. Your epected money is tracked from the debtor,s pockets until you get it fully paid. You also get to have a complete logbook of all debts and debtors on one platform with due reminders.

  • There is absolutely no cost for our platform. It is totally free.

  • The tracking of debts, reminders, contacting debtors will be fully handled by our financial and accountability team. There is little or no involvement for you as long as the situation is well balanced.

  • You can set timelines for payments to be in done in ways as agreed between you and your debtor.

  • To make the process faster, you should link your bank account to your debtor site. However you choose, payments will be vetted through our security team.

  • Transcripts of all debts and credits will be provided to you monthly or yearly to suit your choice. This is to help you have an official information as regards payments and cleared or resoluted debts.
